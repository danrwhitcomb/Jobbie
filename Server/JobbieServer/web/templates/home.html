What is Jobbie?
Jobbie is a personalized job search experience. We pull from over 20,000 job listings to find the right jobs for you. You could spend hours searching through job boards, and may only end up with one or two jobs you're interested in. Jobbie removes the repetition and frustration from your job search. Jobbie makes it manageable and fun.
Just enter some quick information, and you'll be seeing job listings that are chosen just for you
Match the jobs you want to keep for later, and reject the ones you don't want to see again. Jobbie will keep track of both
As you sort through jobs, Jobbie learns your likes and dislikes. Using this it can personalize your results even further
